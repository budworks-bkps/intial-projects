April 2026

Markets don’t wait.

And neither do we.

We’ve completed a fresh review of WealthEdge PMS and we’re making measured changes to keep your portfolio aligned with where we see opportunity today.

First, where we stand.

Over the past year, the portfolio has delivered 13.2% alpha, navigating volatility across asset classes.

Our approach remains unchanged: high conviction, high active share, and a sharp focus on risk-adjusted, consistent returns.

Now, what changes for you. A gradual and deliberate repositioning. You may see new names added, and some positions trimmed over time. We evaluate with care—balancing conviction, cost (taxes, exit loads), and timing.

The goal is simple: not to move fast, but to move right.

Deeper conviction in India

We are increasing exposure to mid and small-cap companies.

Why?
Because the next phase of growth—across consumption, manufacturing, and financials is emerging beyond just large caps.

At the same time, we are reducing US equity exposure. India today offers a strong, visible opportunity set—without the added complexity of currency and global macro risks.

Commodities, with clarity

We’ve simplified our stance.

Gold continues to act as a reliable stabiliser in uncertain markets.

We’ve exited silver as its industrial nature doesn’t serve the role we need in the portfolio.

Adding discipline through structure

For the first time, we’ve introduced a rules-based allocation.
This focuses on companies that:

  • Have historically delivered strong returns
  • Show lower price volatility

In a market that swings on sentiment, this brings stability and composure to the portfolio.

A tactical opportunity

We’ve added a measured position in public sector banks.

After years of balance sheet clean-up, these institutions are stronger than they’ve been in over a decade, and yet valuations haven’t fully caught up.

That gap is where we see opportunity.

We continue to monitor closely, act thoughtfully, and stay aligned with your long-term goals.

If this sparks a question or a thought, we’re always available to go deeper.
